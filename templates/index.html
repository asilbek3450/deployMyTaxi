About
Taxi Company

Taxi Company means any person or entity operating one or more vehicles for hire, other than as a driver, regardless of the legal form of the entity and regardless of whether the taxis so operated are owned by the company, or leased, or owned by individual members of an entity. Taxi Companies do not include TNCs.

MyTaxi

MyTaxi is an online platform to book taxi on-demand in Uzbekistan. Taximeter calculates the fare price and the provides the receipt via the app. User can track the location of the car on the map. User is required to rate the journey, which affects the overall score of the driver. The app is available for both iOS and Android platform.
